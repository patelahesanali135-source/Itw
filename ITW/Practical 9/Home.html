LDRP Institute of Technology & Research, established in 2005 in Gandhinagar, is a premier engineering and management college affiliated with Kadi Sarva Vishwavidyalaya. The institute offers quality education in engineering, MCA, and MBA programs, with modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections.
